TIC TAC TOE

Instructions

  1. First Player starts as Player X and Second Player as Player O;
  2. When you press the START button a table with 9 spaces appears;
  3. When the Player X click on a square the "X" appears, then the Player O chooses where to put the "O" and so;
  4. The player who makes a VERTICAL/HORIZONTAL OR DIAGONAL row with three noughts or crosses is the winner and the game is over, in case of draw, the game must be restarted.

Winner is Player X
